Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 20/09/2025

DictX ("the App", "we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how your information is handled when you use our mobile application.

01

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII). The App does not require you to create an account, and we do not track your personal data.

02

Local Data Storage

All notes, favorites, and history created within the App are stored locally on your device. This information is fully under your control and will not be transmitted to our servers. You may back up or delete this data at your discretion.

03

Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party analytics or advertising services at this time. However, it may display content from external sources (e.g., dictionary websites) which have their own privacy policies.

04

Children’s Privacy

The App is designed for users of all ages and does not collect any personal data. As such, it complies with applicable children’s privacy regulations, including the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

05

Changes to This Policy

We may revise or update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any updates will be posted here with an updated effective date.

06

Contact Us
